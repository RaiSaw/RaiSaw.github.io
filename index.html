About Me

I've been sketching and doodling since I was a kid and finally started focusing on my art these last few years. I'm still experimenting and exploring different mediums to express my art.

My paintings are representations of myself; emotions, thoughts and experiences. My works are mostly mixtures of surrealism and figurative with a touch of nature, space or fantasy. Art is not just a form of expression but as well meditation for me.
